# Admin Panel System Requirements Document (SRS)

## 1. Project Overview

This document defines the functional requirements for developing an **Admin Panel** that manages **Seed Operations (Storage & Packaging Movement)** along with **HRMS and Payroll**. The system is designed for **manual data entry only**, with a focus on **compliance, traceability, and simplicity**.

The solution will be used internally by the organization and does **not require public signup, login, or data upload features**.

---

## 2. Objectives

* Maintain accurate records of seed storage, packaging, and movement
* Track stock at batch and location level
* Manage employees, attendance, and payroll
* Generate audit-ready movement and payroll records
* Ensure simplicity for operational staff and inspectors

---

## 3. System Modules

### 3.1 Seed & Batch Management

* Manual entry of:

  * Crop
  * Variety / Hybrid
  * Batch Number
  * Lot Size
  * Production Date
* Each batch acts as the base unit for stock tracking

---

### 3.2 Location Management

* Create and manage locations:

  * Storage
  * Packaging
  * Processing
  * Sale
* Each location includes:

  * Location name
  * Location type
  * Capacity (optional)

---

### 3.3 Storage Stock Register

* Manual stock-in entry
* Fields:

  * Batch number
  * Crop & variety
  * Storage location
  * Quantity (kg / bags)
  * Entry date
  * Responsible employee

---

### 3.4 Storage to Packaging Movement

* Controlled stock movement form
* Required fields:

  * From storage location
  * To packaging unit
  * Batch number
  * Quantity moved
  * Date
  * Remarks
* System validation:

  * Cannot move quantity greater than available stock
  * Automatic stock deduction from source location

---

### 3.5 Packaging Output Management

* Manual entry after packaging completion
* Fields:

  * Batch number
  * Packet size
  * Number of packets produced
  * Waste / loss quantity (if any)
* System behavior:

  * Finished stock auto-calculated
  * Packaging stock updated accordingly

---

### 3.6 Movement & Compliance Register

* Auto-generated log for all stock movements
* Captures:

  * Date & time
  * From location → To location
  * Quantity
  * Batch number
  * Employee responsible
* Read-only for audit and inspection purposes

---

## 4. HRMS Module

### 4.1 Employee Master

* Manual employee entry
* Fields:

  * Employee name
  * Employee ID
  * Role / designation
  * Department
  * Assigned work location
  * Salary type (monthly / daily)

---

### 4.2 Attendance & Shift Management

* Manual daily attendance entry
* Attendance types:

  * Present
  * Absent
  * Half day
* Shift allocation:

  * Day shift
  * Night shift
  * Packaging shift

---

### 4.3 Role & Responsibility Mapping

* Employees linked to:

  * Storage handling
  * Packaging operations
  * Administrative tasks
* Used for accountability in movement logs

---

## 5. Payroll Module

### 5.1 Salary Structure Configuration

* Define salary components:

  * Basic salary / daily wage
  * Allowances
  * Overtime rate
  * Deductions (leave, advances, penalties)

---

### 5.2 Payroll Processing

* Payroll auto-calculated using:

  * Attendance records
  * Overtime entries
  * Deduction rules
* Monthly payroll generation

---

### 5.3 Payslip & Payroll Reports

* Generate payslips (PDF / printable format)
* Payroll summaries:

  * Employee-wise
  * Department-wise
  * Month-wise

---

## 6. Dashboards & Reports

* Admin dashboard showing:

  * Current stock summary
  * Storage vs packaging stock
  * Active employees
  * Payroll status
* Exportable reports (Excel / PDF):

  * Stock register
  * Movement register
  * Attendance register
  * Payroll reports

---

## 7. Access & Security

* Internal admin-only system
* Role-based access:

  * Admin
  * Operations Manager
  * HR / Payroll
* No public signup or external access

---

## 8. Compliance & Audit Readiness

* Immutable movement logs
* Historical attendance and payroll records
* Batch and location traceability
* Inspector-friendly data views

---

## 9. Non-Functional Requirements

* Web-based admin panel
* Mobile-responsive UI
* Simple forms with validation
* Fast data entry with minimal clicks

---

## 10. Technology Expectations (Suggested)

* Frontend: React / Next.js
* Backend: Node.js / Express
* Database: PostgreSQL / MySQL
* Hosting: Replit-compatible environment

---

## 11. Future Enhancements (Optional)

* Barcode / QR code integration
* Bank and statutory payroll integration
* Role-based approval workflows
